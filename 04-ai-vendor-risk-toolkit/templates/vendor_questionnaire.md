## IAM and Access Control

- Does the product support MFA?

## Data Protection

- Is data encrypted at rest?
- Is data encrypted in transit?
- What is the default data retention period?
- Can customers configure retention?
- Can customers request deletion?
- Where is data stored and processed?

## AI-Specific Questions

- Is customer data used for model training?
- Is customer data used for product improvement?
- Can customers opt out of training or improvement use?
- Are prompts, outputs, files, and metadata logged?
- Are human reviewers able to access customer content?

## Logging and Incident Response

- What audit logs are available?
- How long are audit logs retained?
- Can logs be exported to a SIEM?
- What is the security incident notification SLA?
- How are customers notified of subprocessor changes?
